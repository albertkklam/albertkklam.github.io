Hi there!

My name is Albert and I’m from Sydney. I started verbose: 1 as a way to share some of the things I am working on in the world of statistics. The site’s name is a bit of wordplay on the “verbose” parameter found in most machine learning packages, which controls whether progress messages are printed to the screen. By turning this on, users can track the model fitting process as it happens, and in much the same way, this blog is dedicated to tracking my own learning.

You can also check out what I’ve been reading outside of statistics here.
